# Friction Point Framework

## The Geometric Structure

**Two perpendicular lines, diagonally positioned in a cube:**

```
        Good Boy (up/down axis)

  Naughty ←──┼──→ Naughty
  Boy        |        Boy
```

**Around the edges of this invisible cube, we draw a circle: THE MYTHOLOGICAL LOOP**

This is the prison structure. You move along the circle, traversing between good boy and naughty boy states, but never escaping the loop itself.

The naughty boy is NOT freedom—it's the inverse mythology. You can move from fundamentalist Christianity to satanic circles and you're **still in the same loop**. You haven't escaped. You've just changed position on the circle around the cube.
