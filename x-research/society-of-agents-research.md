## What "Society of Agents" Means in Current AI Discourse

The phrase is widely used as a conceptual framework (not an org name) describing the emerging paradigm of networked AI agents that collaborate, similar to how humans form professional societies. Key sources:

- **Minsky's Society of Mind (1986)** — original philosophical substrate. Never formally instantiated as software. The academic "Society of Agents" (SoA) framework from IGI Global directly inherits this.
- **The real thesis:** AI agents transition from isolated silos to networked collaborators with trust scores, registries, and specialization — mirroring how human professional networks work.
- **February 2026 OffNote Labs newsletter** frames this explicitly: humans become "governors of an agent society," not micromanagers. OpenClaw/Moltbook cited as early instantiations.

## Strategic Read

- An AI-native professional community built around the agentic AI wave (positioning as the "Chamber of Commerce for the agent economy")

## Verified Details on Adjacent Organizations (from source fetches)

### ASFAI (American Society for AI)
- asfai.org
- Invitation-only, C-suite/senior exec only, board vetting required
- Two annual off-the-record summits at undisclosed (Ritz Carlton-caliber) locations
- Board: Michael Carey, Paritosh Ambekar, Michael Groen, Miguel Paredes, Terry Virts, Rei Llazani, Ramine Roane, Paul Daugherty, Lena Smart, Elizabeth Ngonzi, Matthew Guggemos, Will Hatcher, Aaron Poynton

### Advanced AI Society (advancedaisociety.org)
- Formerly: "Decentralized AI Society"
- Chairman: Michael Casey
- Mission: "Ensure everyone has access to an AI stack that preserves autonomy" — privacy, portability, verifiability
- Focus: "Proof-of-Control" standards for AI governance
- Members announced February 2026 (was in stealth)
- Edge & Node is a founding member
- Davos (WEF) presence Jan 2025
- MIT Media Lab partnership on agent registries
- No chapter structure
- No Nevada connection

### OffNote Labs / "Towards Society of Agents" piece

- Published: February 17, 2026
- Technical piece, not an org announcement
- Describes technical infrastructure for multi-agent coordination: Git + filesystem as shared blackboard
- OpenClaw/Moltbook referenced as early instantiations of agent societies
- No chapter model, no geographic angle, no Nevada connection

### Walnut AI / CES 2026 "Social Frontier: Agents Meet First"

- Concept: AI "digital twins" doing pre-screening "Agent Handshakes" for professional networking
